About BishopPair

BishopPair is a website that allows a user to play a 2v2 chess match live against other players. On each turn, both players on each team have 15 seconds to select a move. After this 15 seconds, the engine will evaluate both moves and play the one it evaluates as the best on their behalf. As of now, there is no rating system, although if the app gets popular enough I may consider implementing one in the future.
